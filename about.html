Our Story

Discover the passion behind Cut & Style

Established in 2010

Cut & Style was founded with a simple mission: to provide premium grooming services in a welcoming, masculine environment. Our founder, James Wilson, wanted to create a space where men could relax, enjoy quality service, and leave looking their best.

Our Philosophy

We believe that a great haircut is more than just a service - it's an experience. Our barbers are trained in both classic and contemporary techniques, ensuring you get the perfect cut every time.

The Team

Our team consists of 4 master barbers with over 50 years of combined experience. Each barber brings their unique style and expertise, but all share our commitment to excellence.
